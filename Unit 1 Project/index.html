Welcome to my blog!

My name is Stefano and I like planes! If you are interested in aviation, and especially aviation photography, you've come to the right place. I started to take pictures of planes very recently when I got some professional DSLR gear. Now my SD card is (probably) about 5% pictures of planes out of the 5,000+ photos on my card.

I also enjoy watching planes on FlightRadar24, which I got an account for when I became interested in planes. It's a great pastime. it also allows me to gather information on planes that are flying overhead, which I (and others around me) find interesting.

I live near Toronto Lester B. Pearson Int. Airport (CYYZ), so I mainly spot for planes coming in and out of Pearson. I have visited a few times just to take photos of planes, with my favourite spot being Convair Drive.

I always await the Emirates A380 (flight EK242) that leaves Pearson almost every day except Tuesdays. I love seeing the massive jet fly overhead!
